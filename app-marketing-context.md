## Value Proposition

- **Problem:** Transferring files between different OS devices is painful. AirDrop only works Apple-to-Apple. Nearby Share only works Android. USB cables are outdated. Emailing files to yourself is embarrassing. Cloud services require internet and are slow for large files.
- **Target Audience:** Multi-device users who own a mix of Apple/Windows/Android devices and regularly need to move files between them. Power users, creative professionals, students, office workers.
- **Unique Differentiator:** Best-in-class UI/UX design among cross-platform file transfer tools. Consumer-grade polished experience vs. developer-tool aesthetic of competitors (LocalSend). Apple Vision Pro support. Zero-config auto-discovery. 200%+ faster transfer speeds.
- **Elevator Pitch:** Airclap is the AirDrop that works on everything — drop files between Mac, iPhone, Windows, and Android over local WiFi with zero setup, zero internet, and beautiful design.

### Core Value Pillars

1. **Universal** — True cross-platform: Mac, iOS, Windows, Android, Vision Pro
2. **Instant** — Zero-config auto-discovery, drag & drop, transfer in seconds
3. **Private** — LAN-only, no cloud, no server, no data collection
4. **Beautiful** — Consumer-grade design quality in a utility category

## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Threat

| App | Type | Price | Strengths | Weaknesses | Threat |
|-----|------|-------|-----------|------------|--------|
| **LocalSend** | Direct | Free, open-source | Zero cost, 35k+ GitHub stars, developer trust | Rough UI, no commercial support, no Vision Pro | **Critical** |

### Secondary Competitors

| App | Type | Price | Strengths | Weaknesses | Threat |
|-----|------|-------|-----------|------------|--------|
| **PairDrop** | Direct | Free, open-source | Browser-based, no install | Unstable >200MB, trust issues post-LimeWire acquisition | Medium |
| **AirDrop** | Indirect | Free (built-in) | Zero friction, system-level | Apple ecosystem only | Low |
| **Quick Share** | Indirect | Free (built-in) | Android native, Google-backed | Google/Android only | Low |
| **KDE Connect** | Indirect | Free, open-source | Feature-rich (notifications, clipboard) | Complex, not focused | Low |
| **WeTransfer** | Indirect | Freemium ($12/mo) | High brand awareness | Requires internet, slow, 2GB limit | Low |

### Competitive Strategy

- **Don't compete on price** — LocalSend is free and open-source, you can't win there
- **Compete on experience** — Airclap is the "iPhone of file transfer tools": premium design, polished UX
- **Compete on simplicity** — Zero-config auto-discovery, true drag-and-drop, no QR codes or pairing
- **Compete on speed** — 200%+ faster than alternatives (v2.3 claim)
